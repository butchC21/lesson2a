Weather Site Name

The name of the site is "Meteorological Station".

Site Purpose

The purpose of Meteorological Station includes the following:

1. Monitor ambient meteorological conditions.
2. Get weather information in real time.
3. The site can function on multiple devices at once, and by multiple members of the family.
4. It can help you, and your family members stay safe the moment the weather changes are detected.
5. It gives real-time alerts thus help you and your family members be informed about the weather, helping everyone stay safer.
6. It can help you be more well prepared for the weather in the future days and can help you plan around this better.

Domain Name
