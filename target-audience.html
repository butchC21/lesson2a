Audience

All individuals whether outdoor enthusiasts, a regular employee, students, parents, children, or a thrill-seekers. In short, anyone who wants to know the weather forecast today till the next five days. Most of our visitors can access it from their mobile device, desktop, or tablet.

Two Personas

Mark Valdez

Mark is a 21-year-old male who likes sports and outdoor activities. He wants to hike with his friends during the weekend to relax and relieve from stress on his day-to-day work. He heard on the news that there is an upcoming typhoon. So, he contacted his friends on what their plans are or if the said typhoon will really hit their place. As such he checks the Meteorological Station of the 5-day weather forecast so he and his friends will know if they are going to pursue their plan or not.

Cara Miguel

Cara is a 42-year-old outdoor enthusiast female, and she likes to enjoy her weekend fishing with her husband and kids. She loves to go to the lake nearby their house and spend the whole day fishing. Aside from that she also enjoys hunting wild animals together with her husband. Prior to their fishing she will check on her mobile phone if there will be any upcoming storm or hurricane that can hamper their plans.

Scenarios

I want to go to the beach with my family and friends tomorrow, and I want to check the weather condition?
The meteorological station will show you the weather forecast for 5 days.

Can I access a map?
Yes, you can access a map on the website which is located at the bottom.

Is the website responsive to different portals such as desktop, tablet, or phone?
Yes, the website can be viewed and accessed on desktop, tablet, or phone.

Will there be news articles regarding the weather and other geological updates?
Yes, there will be news and weather article summaries on a regular basis.
